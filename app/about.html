My Story and Journey

Hi! I am Serghei Iakovlev, a software engineer and tech enthusiast from Wrocław, Poland.

My Current Work

At present, I am employed at airSlate, where I develop a comprehensive online platform for automating business processes. This platform enables users to create, fill out, and sign documents in real time.

airSlate provides tools for building and customizing business workflows without writing any code, allowing for accelerated processes and increased productivity.

It facilitates the automation of various business operations, such as contract management, loan application processing, document approvals, and much more.

Experience

With the Phalcon

At In the past, I was the primary maintainer of the Zephir language and was part of the Phalcon team. Together, we developed a full-featured PHP framework delivered as a C extension. Currently, I have ended my association with PHP and Zephir. I prototype in Python, dabble in Lisp, and occasionally work with C.

Areas of Focus

My primary focus is on programming language development, creating language parsers, working with static and semantic analysis, and conducting research in compiler development — advanced analysis and optimization for high-performance computing. For many years, I have been dedicated to creating and enhancing tools that make life easier for developers, enabling them to use their time more efficiently.

Personal Interests

In my free time (which is not often), I enjoy watching movies, reading, hiking, and cycling.
